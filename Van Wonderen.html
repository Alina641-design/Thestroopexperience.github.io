Van Wonderen

About:

Located in the heart of Amsterdam, Van Wonderen Stroopwafels is famous for its beautifully‑presented, freshly‑made stroopwafels — thin waffle discs filled with gooey caramel and topped with creative coatings from melted chocolate to colorful sprinkles. The shop captures the essence of Dutch indulgence with flair and flavour.

The origins of the brand trace back to a family legacy: the founder’s grandfather, Gerardus van Wonderen (born 9 August 1907), laid the groundwork, and the modern shop honours this heritage. Today, Van Wonderen combines tradition with a contemporary twist — inviting both locals and visitors to enjoy a sweet treat that reflects over a century of family passion for baking.

A Look Inside the Store:

Each stroopwafel is baked fresh right in front of you, creating that perfect caramel aroma that fills the store. Watch as warm syrup is spread between two crispy layers — the signature Dutch delight.

Beautifully Wrapped Souvenirs:

Van Wonderen is known for its elegant packaging — from gift boxes to tin cans featuring Amsterdam’s charm. They make the perfect sweet souvenir to take home or share with friends.

A Sweet Amsterdam Memory:

Whether you stop by for a quick treat or to collect gifts, Van Wonderen captures the warmth, craft, and joy of Amsterdam in every bite.

Perfect Pairings:

Enjoy your stroopwafel with a hot coffee or tea — just place it on top of your cup and let the caramel soften for the ultimate Dutch experience.
